Introduction to this application

  • All the items are taken manually.

  • Fixed sales tax of 10% is applicable to all products except books, food and medical products.

  • Duty tax of 5% is applicable if any item is imported, with no exemptions.

  • After adding items, click view your cart to view cart items and print receipt.

  • Categories of the items are assumed to be fixed to calculate tax.
